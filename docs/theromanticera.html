The Romantic Era

Summary

My other passion is music. I have been playing piano since the age of 7, and I’ve been in a band (The Romantic Era) for the last 10 years. In addition to my contributions to the creation of our music, I also create any accompanying graphics including album artwork, photos, and music videos. While it’s not strictly User Experience work, the design I have done for the band displays my knack for creating aesthetically pleasing artwork.

Logo

A few years ago, I decided to refresh our logo. The heart was played out and bit ‘on the nose’ for a band called The Romantic Era. I’m not sure of exactly when it started, but some of our rowdier fans took to repeatedly chanting “T, R, E” at our shows. This morphed into a nickname of TRE (pronounced like “tray”) for the band and often throwing up 3 fingers in a tribute to the chant that started it all. 3 became symbolic for us, and the refreshed logo was a representation of a new era for the band.
