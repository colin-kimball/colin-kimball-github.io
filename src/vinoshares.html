Summary

VinoShares is a startup that I have been working on outside of my job at Erie Insurance. The idea of VinoShares is to make expensive and hard to obtain wines available by the glass instead of the bottle. Often times expensive bottles are only available for purchase by the bottle. Our solution makes all bottles available by the glass, by offering shares (5oz pours) of the wine. This is made possible when a bottle is successfully crowdsourced by the VinoShares community (meaning that 5 shares must be bought). Because of our crowdsourcing method, a bottle can be opened and consumed in one night to prevent deterioration of the product. VinoShares partners with local restaurant hosts to hold events where consumers can come and enjoy their wine shares.

Project Goal: To build a progressive web app for users to view and purchase shares to be consumed at events in their area. Basically, we had to build this platform from the ground up.
