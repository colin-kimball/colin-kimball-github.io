About

I am a User Experience Designer from Erie, Pennsylvania. With a BA in Graphic Design, I bring a strong sense of visual design, coupled with a aptitude and passion for front-end development.

As a former college football player, I bring skills of hard-work, teamwork and leadership to my work. Coaching and mentoring those around me has become a passion of mine. During 4+ years working at a Fortune 500 company, I have designed simple applications that deliver strong business value.
